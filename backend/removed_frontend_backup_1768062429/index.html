About TimelinePlus

TimelinePlus is a legal social media marketing services platform connecting buyers and workers through manual task completion and secure OAuth linking.

We built TimelinePlus to provide transparent and manual social engagement services that respect platform policies and user privacy. Buyers can create campaigns with clear task requirements and Workers can complete tasks and submit proof. Admins review all submissions to ensure eligibility and honest payouts.

All features are manual and human-driven; automation, scraping, or credential sharing is strictly prohibited. We use OAuth read-only linking where available to verify accounts without ever storing your passwords.

Terms of Service

By using TimelinePlus, you agree to follow our community and platform rules. You must not use bots, scraping tools, or share account credentials. Tasks must be completed manually and truthfully.

TimelinePlus reserves the right to suspend or ban accounts that violate these terms, or that otherwise attempt to manipulate outcomes unfairly. Withdrawals are subject to admin review and may be delayed for verification.

Privacy Policy

We collect minimal personal information required to operate the service, such as email, username, and transaction records. When you link a social account using OAuth, we only request read-only profile information necessary to validate proofs.

We never ask for or store your social account passwords. Your data is used for platform operation, payments, and support; we do not share personal data with third parties except when required by law.
